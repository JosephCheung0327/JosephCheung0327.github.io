Courses

This is a list of courses I have taken at UofT, and some information about them. I am aiming to complete a CS Specialist and a Statistics Major. I hope this will be useful for future students pursuing similar majors. Note: I transferred 2.5 credits from IB (unfortunately all of them are BR5).

The courses are arranged according to their course code, in alphabetical order.

Year 1 (2024-2025)

CSC110: Foundations of Computer Science I (Paul He & Sadia Sharmin)
BR5, Course Average: A-
This is a well-structured course on the basics of CS and Python. For those who studied CS in high school, this course is a great review but some of you might find it boring. For those new to programming, this course is paced appropriately, and you have lots of support from the teaching team. This is a full-credit course, so there are more lecture hours than other courses. The tests and exams are straightforward, so many students get (crazily) amazing scores.
